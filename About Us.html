ABOUT McINROY CORNER CAFE



McInroy Corner Cafe (MCC) is a university campus cafeteria
whose main headquarters are located in St.Joseph and provide daily lunches,
snacks and drinks to campus staff and students.

This university cafeteria combines both delectable, affordable
cuisine with a welcoming atmosphere as well as friendly and professional staff who
can cater to your every need and want in a timely manner.
Executive Chef Oji Ashebre Jaja incorporates Caribbean cuisine in every menu since the business' inception.
The staff is also very knowledgable on every meal served at this fine establishment.
